Amplify Reading

Amplify Reading is a personalized reading program that students can’t wait to play. Whether they are just learning to read or mastering close reading, Amplify Reading helps achieve off-the-charts reading growth in grades K–8.

My Contributions

Designed interaction, UI, and animation for 48 quests, 7 minigames, 3 hubs, 2 eReader projects end-to-end to make enjoyable experiences for K-5 students while satisfying pedagogical needs for improving their early literacy skills.

Platform:

Web, iOS, Chromebook

Design Challenges:

  • Due to budget and deadline constraints, we had to design one version for all platforms
  • Given that we were using React Native to make games, our design decisions were very restricted
  • Our end-users were young kids who haven't developed technological prowess
  • Student in their early ages develop core skills quickly, forcing us to have different product versions for at least every 2 grade levels
  • Even in the same grade, student learn in different speed, so we had to make out product adapative to their level and pace
  • We had to satisfy varity customers, not only to make it fun for kids, but also need to demonstrat the educational value to teachers and parents
  • In order to sell our products in certain state, we were required by law to make our product accessible and meet WCAG 2.0 AA standards
