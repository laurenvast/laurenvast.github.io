About Lauren_

Lauren sometimes is an artsy geek, and sometimes she's a geeky artist. She's currently working at Amplify Education to make enjoyable experiences for kids to improve their early literacy skills.

Lauren is a Design Technologist and mainly works in the intersection between design, technology and art. For personal projects, she cares about people's emotion and tries to add feeling to the digital world built with emotionless code. For business projects, she always seeks to improve upon the process of making purposeful + implementable design, and delivering functional + aesthetic front-end experience.
