About_

Lauren is a Designer also a Technologist specialized in interaction design, UI design, dynamic web animation, and prototyping in code. She's currently working at Amplify Education to make enjoyable experiences for kids to improve their early literacy skills.

For business projects, she always seeks to improve upon the process of making purposeful + implementable design, and delivering functional + aesthetic front-end experience. For independent projects, she tries to push the boundry of technical limitation and make people ask "How did you do that?!"
