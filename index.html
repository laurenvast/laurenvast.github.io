About Lauren_

Lauren sometimes is an artsy geek, and sometimes she's a geeky artist. She graduated from Pratt Institute with an MFA degree in Interactive Arts.

Lauren is a multidisciplinary designer and mainly works in the intersection between design, technology and art. She's passionate about creating digital experiences with design + code. She also cares about people's emotion and tries to add feelings to the world built with cold code.
