VR Kangdinsky

Statement

VR Kandinsky is an VR application that allows users to explore a Kandinsky’s artwork in 3D space.

Concept

Our eyes are easy to trick, and what we see from one point of view might not be true. Reconstructing a famous painting in a 3D space in a way that is unexpected conveys this idea. Every individual element can be interact and affect other objects, and users don’t know how the changes they made appears on the original painting point of view. This states the notion that one cannot see the whole picture when they’re inside of it.

Tech used

Unity, Google cardboard API
