but_

Statement

But is an interactive object that reveals the distance between our expectations and the reality. It allows audience to write the poem collaboratively by sending a text message to a specific number with a word “but”. The message will be read and shown on the screen embedded in a frame hanged on a wall instantly.

Concept

Every line of the poem is composed by two parts and logically connected with a “but”, and it shows how the second part contradict the first part. The first four lines stay on the top while the rest keeps updating. With this setting, the audience are able to read the same original poem, and to contrast how it is different than the part that people right before them have written. This difference in writing style and tone of the words reveal how it fails the artist’s expectation on the poem and therefore shows that the reality beats the expectation.

Tech used

JavaScript, Gmail API, Project Fi
