Statement

Smart ears is a wearable device that indicates the wearer’s emotional state in relation to other people.

Concept

Some people may more shy than others, but in the same time, they want to express themselves. Smart ears’ movement is affected by the distance between the wearer and people in front of them. The closer it gets, the ears become more excited. When no one’s around, it’s on the calm mode.

Tech used

Arduino uno, servo motors, kinetic connection
