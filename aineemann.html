Aimee Mann Website

Launch Site

Background

Aimee Mann is an american singer/song-writer, and "Mental Illness" is her first album in five years. The goal of the splash page is to promote this new album.

Inspiration

I took inspiration from her album artwork, used the tree and the goose as main elements, and made the fixed parallax effect that react to mouse position to have a sense of wind

Technology

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, photoshop
